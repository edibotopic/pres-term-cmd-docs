4 Screenshots
--------------------------------------

Pros: can show exactly what the user sees
Cons: unnecessary info, accessibility issues, standards overhead, maintenance issues

5 Terminal image generators
--------------------------------------

Pros: more consistent and clear than screenshots
Cons: accessibility issues, maintenance issues

6 Auto-generated images
--------------------------------------

Examples: freeze, carbon CLI

Pros: compatible with CI pipelines
Cons: accessibility issues

7 Screen recordings
--------------------------------------

Pros: saves visual space, developer personality
Cons: size, accessibility, maintenance, no copy

8 Video as text
--------------------------------------

Pros: saves visual space, can be copied
Cons: user needs to understand the technology

9 Video as code
--------------------------------------

Pros: saves visual space, compatible with CI 
Cons: no copy, can't be printed

10 Interactive CLI
--------------------------------------

Pros: sandboxed environment
